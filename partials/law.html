The needs of law enforcement and military units are different from civilian self-defense, as well as being different from each other. At Krav Fit, we provide specific training to meet the needs of both military and law enforcement personnel.

Krav Maga was originally developed in Israel for military and law enforcement and it was based on violent encounters and combat situations that these personnel faced. We recognize that policies and tactics differ in other countries as well as in the United States, especially between different organizations and units, so we work hard to adapt Krav Maga techniques to the needs of individual agencies while maintaining the efficient, effective nature of the Krav Maga system. Krav Maga has been adopted by hundreds of U.S. law enforcement agencies because it is simple, effective, and adaptable for agents of all ages and abilities. The techniques are easy to retain and can be performed under extreme stress.

The Krav Maga system has been praised by defensive tactics instructors from federal, state, and local law enforcement agencies around the country as well as combat trainers from special units of the military.
